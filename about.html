About Our Project

Our Carbon Emission Prediction and Analysis platform aims to provide insights into the historical carbon emissions of countries and forecast their future emissions using AI and data analytics. By understanding past patterns and predicting future impacts, we hope to raise awareness, influence policy decisions, and contribute towards a sustainable planet.

This project uses machine learning regression models trained on environmental datasets to provide reliable forecasts, coupled with interactive data visualizations for clarity and impact.
